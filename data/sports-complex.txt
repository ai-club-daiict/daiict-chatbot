Sports Complex
Vision
All students will be physically educated, have motor skills to perform a variety of physical activities and pursue a healthy and active lifestyle in an increasingly changing world.
In DAIICT sports and games are an important and unique part of the daily programme. Not only does it contribute to the overall goals of education, but also contributes to the development and maintenance of fitness, motor skills, social skills and health. The sports department of our institute has always endeavoured to fulfil the basic objectives of sports and games by providing the appropriate infrastructural facilities and the right kind of motivation to aspiring students. Our institute produced many outstanding sports persons who have brought laurels to our institution in various Intra and extramural competitions.DAIICT has been taking part in the various District, State and National level tournaments in Athletics, Table Tennis, Badminton, BasketBall,Tennis,Cricket,Volleyball,Chess and Football. As per the annual sports policy of the institution all sports and games coaching sessions are divided in to two phases
PHASE-I=From January to March.
PHASE-II=From August to October.
Infrastructure
Basketball courts -02, Athletic track(400mtrs) -01, Indoor Badminton courts -03, Cricket field -01, Gymnasium with all modern facilities-01, Football ground-01,Volleyball Courts -02, Table Tennis Hall equipped with six tables - 01, Yoga and meditation zone.
