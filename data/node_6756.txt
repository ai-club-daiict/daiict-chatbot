Research Fellow position for project CSR-25/BSES/A9-AJ/SELC
14 May, 2025
Research Fellow
:
Smart Energy Learning Centre
(A New Research Centre at DA-IICT funded by BSES Delhi, India)
Reference Number:
CSR-25/BSES/A9-AJ/SELC
Applications are invited from highly motivated candidates with good academic records for a full-time, fixed-term Research Fellow position with the Smart Energy Learning Centre (SELC) at the Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DA-IICT), India. The project details, requisite qualifications, and experience are as follows.
Project Title
Energy Efficient and Reliable Solutions to Monitoring, Data Collection, and Security of Non Centralized Wireless Mesh Networks: Routing Protocols and Coding Scheme
Position and Fellowship
Research Fellow –
1 post
INR 45,000 per month
Eligibility Requirements
Essential Qualifications
B.E./B.Tech. in Electronics and communication Engineering, ICT or equivalent, with at least 70% marks.
Strong command of oral and written English.
Desirable Qualifications
Proficiency with programming and modeling software (e.g. MATLAB, Python)
Period of Appointment:
This position is temporary, with an initial appointment for one year, and it is extendable for up to two more years based on satisfactory performance. This does not confer any right for the candidate to claim an extension or absorption at DA-IICT.
Upper Age
35 Years as on the date of Application.
How to Apply:
Interested candidates must send their detailed
Curriculum Vitae
with a Cover Letter by
May 20, 2025
through an email to PIs at
abhishek_jindal[at]daiict[dot]ac[dot]in
&
manish_kumar[at]daiict[dot]ac[dot]in
.
Please write “
Application for Research Fellow position for project CSR-25/BSES/A9-AJ/SELC
” in the subject line of the email.
Only shortlisted candidates will be called for an interview, which they will attend at their own expense; no TA/DA will be provided. For any queries, the candidates can reach out to PIs at the above mentioned email IDs.
Tentative date of Interview:
Week starting from May 21, 2025 (Hybrid/ Online)
Expected start date:
May 25, 2025 or after.
