Assistant Manager – Alumni Office
Dhirubhai Ambani University (DAU)
is seeking a dynamic
Assistant Manager for the Alumni Office
, responsible for developing and implementing strategies to build and nurture relationships between the university and its nearly ten thousand graduates, friends, students, well-wishers, and donors. The primary objective is to maximize alumni and donor interest in and engagement with DAU.
Duties:
Organizes and coordinates alumni events such as local chapter meetings, alumni backed outreach activities, weekend campus visits to reach particular constituent groups.
Builds alumni membership though personal contacts, events, and presentations to community groups. Develops e-solicitation or mail solicitation projects including idea, design, feedback approval process, and implementation. Processed alumni identity cards.
Oversees alumni chapter budgets (raising sponsorships, budget etc.), meetings and activities.
Collaborates with alumni affairs colleagues in other offices on campus to develop strategies, goals and objectives for alumni participation.
Creates university-community partnerships by recruiting business and community leaders and encouraging continued involvement through group participation.
Makes contacts within and outside DAU to obtain new alumni benefits and increase the value of DAU Alumni Office membership.
Qualification, Experience & Skill Requirements:
A postgraduate degree in any discipline.
Minimum 7 to 10 years
Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
Proven organizational and event management capabilities.
Proficiency in digital marketing and social media.
